# Your First Meeting With SVG Agency — The Fact Finder

## Welcome

Thank you for scheduling time with SVG Agency. This video walks you through what to expect in our first meeting and what to bring so we can make the most of our time together.

## What Is a Fact Finder?

A fact finder is our first real conversation about your company's benefit program. It's not a sales pitch — it's a discovery session. We need to understand where you are today before we can show you where you could be.

Think of it like a doctor's visit: before any treatment plan, we need a diagnosis. The fact finder is the diagnosis.

## What We'll Cover

### Your Company
- Company name, address, and EIN
- Number of employees (full-time, part-time)
- Industry and type of work
- Years in business
- Growth plans or changes on the horizon

### Your Current Benefits
- What plans do you offer today? (Medical, dental, vision, life, disability, retirement)
- Who is your current broker or advisor?
- When do your plans renew?
- What are you spending today on employee benefits?
- Are you fully insured, self-insured, or level funded?

### Your Pain Points
- What's working well with your current program?
- What's frustrating you?
- Have you had compliance issues or audit concerns?
- Are employees satisfied with their benefits?
- Is recruiting and retention affected by your benefits package?

### Your Goals
- What would your ideal benefits program look like?
- Are you trying to reduce costs, improve coverage, or both?
- Any specific concerns about compliance (ACA, ERISA, COBRA)?
- Are you planning any changes to your workforce?

## What to Bring

To make the most of our meeting, please bring or have available:

1. **Current plan documents** — Summary Plan Descriptions (SPDs) for each benefit
2. **Most recent renewal** — premium rates, carrier quotes, plan designs
3. **Census data** — employee list with dates of birth, hire dates, and coverage elections
4. **Claims experience** — if available, your loss runs or claims reports from the last 2-3 years
5. **Most recent Form 5500** — if you file one (plans with 100+ participants must file)
6. **Current broker agreement** — if you have one, we'd like to understand the relationship

Don't worry if you don't have all of this. We can work with what you have and help you gather the rest.

## How Long Does It Take?

Plan for 45-60 minutes. Some fact finders are shorter if you have everything organized. Some run longer if there's a lot to unpack. There's no rush.

## What Happens After?

After our fact finder, we'll take everything you've shared and build a complete picture of your benefits program. We'll identify:

- **Compliance gaps** — are you meeting all federal and state requirements?
- **Cost opportunities** — are there funding strategies that could save you money?
- **Coverage gaps** — are your employees adequately protected?
- **Market position** — how does your program compare to similar companies in your industry?

This analysis leads to our next meeting, where we'll walk you through an insurance education session and show you exactly how your program stacks up.

## No Pressure, No Obligation

The fact finder is free and carries no obligation. If SVG isn't the right fit, we'll tell you. If your current broker is doing a great job, we'll tell you that too. Our job is to give you clarity — what you do with it is up to you.
