## What We Handle

### Day-to-Day Support
- **Employee questions** — "Is this doctor in network?" "How do I add my new baby?" "What's my deductible?" Your employees can call us directly. We handle it.
- **Claims issues** — Denied claim? Surprise bill? We advocate on behalf of your employees with the carrier. We know the plan, we know the contracts, we fight the fights.
- **Enrollment changes** — New hires, terminations, qualifying life events, COBRA. We process the changes and make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

### Compliance Management
- **Form 5500 filing** — If your plan requires it, we manage the filing process
- **ACA reporting** — 1094-C and 1095-C preparation and filing
- **SPD maintenance** — Keeping your Summary Plan Descriptions current
- **COBRA administration** — Timely notices, election tracking, premium collection
- **ERISA compliance** — Document retention, fiduciary responsibilities, plan amendments
- **Regulatory updates** — When laws change, we tell you what it means for your plan and what you need to do

### Ongoing Analytics
- **Monthly claims monitoring** — We track your claims experience throughout the year, not just at renewal. If something trends wrong, we catch it early.
- **Quarterly reviews** — We sit down with you regularly to review plan performance, claims trends, and any concerns.
- **Benchmarking** — How your plan compares to the market, updated as data changes.
- **Utilization analysis** — Are employees using the benefits? Are high-cost services being managed? Are preventive care programs working?

## The Renewal Process

Renewals don't sneak up on you. Here's how we manage the cycle:

### 120 Days Out
- We pull your updated claims data
- We run fresh Monte Carlo simulations
- We identify market alternatives

### 90 Days Out
- We present the renewal analysis
- We show you options (stay, modify, change carriers, change funding)
- You make decisions with data, not pressure

### 60 Days Out
- We finalize plan designs and carrier selections
- We prepare employee communication materials
- We set up enrollment

### 30 Days Out
- Open enrollment runs
- Employee questions are handled
- Everything is submitted and confirmed before effective date

No last-minute scrambles. No "your renewal is in two weeks and we need a decision today." We start early because surprises are expensive.
